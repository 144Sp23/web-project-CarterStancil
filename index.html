Mission

Our mission is to give women basketball players from the ages of 13 to 22 opportunities that they may not receive anywhere else. We strive to give our members (future basketball stars) the training, coaching, and recovery they need to be successful. We are a one of a kind company that take our members satisfaction very seriously. No ones voice will go unheard as we do our absolute best to help these women reach their goals.

Membership Dues

Dues will be $100 a month per member. This will grant you full access to all of our ammenities which include the courts, gyms, cafeteria, and our rehab centers. Financial assistance is avaiable for those in need. We are a non-profit organization so all dues go directly to the improvement of our facilities.
